MANDE

"What? / Yes?"

A polite way to ask someone to repeat or respond when someone calls you.

Example:

— Leon, ven aquí.
— ¿Mande?

SALE

"Okay / Sounds good"

Used to agree with a plan or accept an idea.

Example:

— Nos vemos a las 6.
— Sale.

CHIDO

"Cool / Awesome"

A very common Mexican word used to describe something you like.

Example:

¡Está muy chido!

AHORITA

"Right now"... maybe

It literally means "right now", but Mexicans also use it to mean "soon".

Example:

Voy ahorita.

ÁNDALE

"Come on!" / "Let's go!"

Used to encourage someone, hurry them up, or show agreement.

Example:

¡Ándale, vámonos!

QUÉ FLOJERA

"I don't feel like it."

Used when something feels boring, tiring, or annoying.

Example:

Tengo que limpiar...
— Qué flojera.

NI MODO

"Oh well."

Used when something can't be changed.

Example:

Perdimos el autobús.
— Ni modo.

NO PASA NADA

"Don't worry."

Used to reassure someone after a mistake or accident.

Example:

Perdón por llegar tarde.
— No pasa nada.
